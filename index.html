// About Thread SL

SIERRA LEONE'S
FASHION
MARKET.

Thread SL is Sierra Leone's first dedicated digital fashion marketplace. We connect buyers with Freetown's most talented designers, tailors, and fabric sellers — from Ankara print specialists in Congo Town to contemporary street wear brands in Lumley.

Whether you want a custom Kente gown for a wedding, a fresh street wear set for everyday, or a hand-crafted accessory from a local artisan — Thread SL is where Sierra Leone shops for fashion.

  • Shop directly from Sierra Leonean designers and tailors
  • Custom orders — send your measurements, get your fit
  • Ankara, Kente, street wear, traditional, and contemporary styles
  • Delivery to all 14 districts and international shipping
  • All prices in Sierra Leonean Leones — no hidden charges
